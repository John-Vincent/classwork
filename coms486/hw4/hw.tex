        \begin{enumerate}[label=(\alph*)]
            \item Probability that A sends frame successfully: $p_A(1 - p_A)$\\
                  Probability that B sends frame successfully: $p_B(1 - p_B)$\\
                  Probability that A or B sends successfully: $(p_A + p_B)(1 - (p_A + p_B))$\\
                  Probability that A and B collide: $p_A(1 - p_A) * p_B(1 - p_B)$\\
                  Probability that neither A or B sends successfully: $1 - (p_A + p_B)(1 - (p_A + p_B))$
            \item A's average throughput is the probability of A sending successfully times the number of frames to send. Since the number
                  of frames is unlimited will just call this $p_A$. For B it is the same situation, we'll call it $p_B$.
        \end{enumerate}
